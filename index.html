OUR MISSION

A Safe Space for authentic leaders (like you), craving to come back to that strong, inspiring and unstoppable version of yourself. Our mission is to help you find a sustainable source of power that will not only get you back into a healthy balance, but will also boost your focus, productivity and joy of life.

While thriving for being a leader, passion can become your kryptonite. Responsibilities, requests from all around and people that depend on your high energy (that, by the way, seems to be never ending to everybody else) – over time even the most resilient humans can drown when pressure takes over purpose.
Coaching at WHO I AM is like your regular vehicle checkup. Instead of running on high and waiting for something to break down, you get an annual oil change and tune-ups to keep your car up and running smoothly and ensure its longevity. Plus, with adding Premium fuel, you even improve its performance. That way, you can save on expensive repairs or long-term damage.
Same goes for Coaching. Enabling you to (recover and) develop within this Safe Space to prevent a burnout that not only affects your career, but your overall life and well-being.

The strongest leaders are those who lead from within.

WHY?

The creation of WHO I AM is intended to be your Safe Space, an isle where you can be 100% your true authentic self - no walls needed. Our purpose is to empower you in all shades of your uniqueness, so that you have the strength and longevity to follow yours. And so, together we make the world a better place.
It all began in 2020 when I first tipped my toes into body and mindset work. As I worked as a leader in Business Development and Sales for many years, I mainly utilized Yoga and Meditation practice for my own health and sanity. One of the key takeaways of the first month into the regular practice was, that it is more than just physical activity.
Curious to deepen my own Yoga practice, I joined a 200h Yoga Teacher Training program. Although I did not know is yet back then, it was the kickoff into my very own FLOW. Through teaching Yoga, I experienced the warmth and contentment that overcame me when working with individuals. Further Trainings and Coachings followed until I created my own Vision.
Today I am grateful to offer you my companionship as a certified Coach.
GREAT LEADERSHIP ISN'T ABOUT POWER - IT IS ABOUT EMPOWERING
I am not a therapist, not a consultant, not a doctor - I am your Coach!
Throughout my career and leadership I have worked myself up to an inch-length away from burnout several times. Unaware about Coaching I drew on my last resources to dig into hours and hours of self reflection, reading books and some therapy sessions. Still I had to make a cut and restart to finally find release. On the way out, I began to analyze causes and individual reasons internally and externally, to unpack my formula of prevention.
As a Certified Systemic Coach and fully certified Breathwork Instructor all I aim for is to help you detect imbalance, find the right path for you and sustainably prevent from severe burnout.
